Overfishing

Did you know that study shows that all the world's fisheries will collapse by 2048 if nothing changes and unsustainable fishing rates continue? Today the share of fish stocks are pushed beyond their biological limits - meaning we catch fish faster than they can reproduce to sustain population levels.
